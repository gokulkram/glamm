# Glamm Hair — Admin Updates Summary

**Date:** 26 June 2026

This document describes the three updates made to the Glamm Hair admin panel and storefront.

---

## 1. Unpublish products (hide without deleting)

You can now **hide a product from your website without deleting it or marking it out of stock.**

**What it does**
- On a product's edit page there is a new **Visibility** section with a **"Published"** switch.
- Turn it **off** and save — the product immediately disappears from your website (home page, shop, and product pages) and cannot be bought.
- All of the product's details (prices, sizes, photos, description) are **kept**. You can switch it back **on** anytime to make it live again.

**Where you see it**
- In the product list, each product shows a **Published** or **Draft** badge so you can tell at a glance what is live and what is hidden.
- The dashboard shows how many products are currently published.

**Good to know**
- "Published" and "In stock" are separate. A product can be hidden (Draft) even if it is in stock, and vice-versa.
- After unpublishing, the home page can take up to a minute to update; the shop and product pages update right away.

---

## 2. Change the order of products

You can now decide **what order your products appear in** — for example, moving a product to be #1 in the list, or #23. The order you set is used on the home page "Top Picks", the shop's "Featured" view, and the admin list.

There are two easy ways to do it:

**a) Type a position number**
- On a product's edit page there is a **"Catalog position"** box.
- Type **1** to make it first, **23** to make it last, or any number in between, then save. Every other product reorders automatically — no gaps or duplicates.

**b) Use the up/down arrows**
- In the product list, each product has small **▲ / ▼** arrows.
- Click to move a product up or down one spot at a time. The change saves instantly.
- (The arrows are turned off while you are searching or filtering the list — clear the search/filter first, since ordering applies to the whole catalog.)

**Good to know**
- The product list now shows a **position number** (#) next to each product, so you always know where it sits.
- The position counts **every** product, including hidden (Draft) ones. So a product at admin position 5 might appear a little higher to customers if a hidden product sits above it.

---

## 3. Bulk Hair shown first

At your request, the catalog order was changed so that **all Bulk Hair products appear first** across the website — on the home page, in the shop's "Featured" view, and in the admin list — followed by the other categories.

**Good to know**
- The home page "Top Picks" shows 8 products. Because you have 9 Bulk Hair products, the home page now shows **all Bulk Hair** there. If you'd prefer the home page to lead with Bulk Hair but still show a mix of other styles, that can be adjusted (for example, showing more products on the home page). Just let us know.
- You can fine-tune the order at any time using the position number or the up/down arrows described above.

---

## Quick how-to

- **Hide a product:** Admin → Products → Edit the product → turn **Published** off → Save.
- **Move a product to a specific spot:** Edit the product → set **Catalog position** (1 = first) → Save.
- **Nudge a product up or down:** Use the **▲ / ▼** arrows in the product list.
